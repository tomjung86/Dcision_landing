About Dcision™

A decision engine for truth‑aligned choices.

What it is

Dcision™ is a new kind of decision system. For businesses, it’s a compliance engine that scans claims, flags risk and assembles evidence. For consumers, it delivers one trusted verdict backed by verified sources and personal values.

How it works

Powered by the TrustGraph™, Dcision checks product language, certifications and disclosures, then explains why something is safe to ship — or not. Outputs are transparent and audit‑ready.
