About Dcision™

The Compliance Engine that prevents expensive surprises.

What It Is

Dcision™ Compliance Engine analyzes product claims and documentation, detects red flags, and maps requirements against standards. Teams get a single, explainable verdict with a “Why this?” trail—before audits do.

Why It Matters

Late-stage compliance failures are costly: product withdrawals, relabelling, fines, and lost brand trust. Dcision™ reduces review cycles and rework by catching vague or non‑compliant claims early and showing exactly what to fix.
