About Dcision™

A compliance‑aware decision engine that reduces risk and rework.

What it is

Dcision™ is a decision engine built on the TrustGraph™. For businesses, it acts as a Compliance Engine that checks claims, flags missing evidence, and generates an explainable verdict you can ship with. For consumers, it delivers one trusted recommendation — aligned to their values — so brands can win on truth, not noise.

Why businesses use it

  • Cut compliance cycle time and cost with automated claim checks & red‑flag detection.
  • Prevent post‑launch withdrawals and fines with explainable “Why this?” evidence packs.
  • Ship faster with confidence — and earn trust with regulators and customers.

Status

Dcision™ is in active development with pilots forming. If you’re an enterprise or marketplace, request a demo to explore a fit.
