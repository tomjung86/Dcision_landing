About Dcision™

One explainable verdict — aligned to standards and your values.

What it is

Dcision™ is a compliance and trust engine. It analyses product claims and documentation, maps evidence to standards, and returns a single verdict with a transparent “Why this?” trail.

Why it matters to business

Late rework, re‑labelling, and withdrawn products are expensive. Dcision flags gaps early, shortens review cycles, and reduces audit surprises—protecting launches and brand equity.

Consumers, too

People are tired of vague marketing and fake reviews. The consumer version invites users to choose based on verified facts and personal values.
